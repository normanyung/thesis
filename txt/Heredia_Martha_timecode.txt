There they taught me for ten months.  I didn’t like it either.  The managers, I didn’t like how they were.  It was good work and everything, because it was big.  I decided to ask for work here because I live here, on 35th Street.  I had always come here to wash my clothes since I lived her, for 18 years.  But till then it had never struck me to ask for work here.  Now I live five minutes from work.  I work five days – in the afternoons on the weekdays and in the mornings on Saturday and Sunday.  I like the customers who come, they are very friendly.  We chat with people.  You don’t get bored.  We have quite a lot of work but I like it.   I only have one sister and distant family in Mexico still.  We are six brothers and sisters, and five of us live here.  And my parents are here.  In the 21 years since I came I have only been back to Mexico two times.  All my family is here.
